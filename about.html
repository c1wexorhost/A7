About Us

MYUNIBRIDGE
The portal for learning partnerships

Myunibridge (www.myunibridge.org) is a premier study abroad and career education advisory, dedicated to helping students gain admission to leading global institutions such as MIT, Columbia, Harvard, LSE, and more. We provide comprehensive guidance for high school, undergraduate, and postgraduate (Masters, MBA, and Executive Education) programs. Our experienced education associates, alumni of top universities, leverage their firsthand knowledge to meticulously guide students through the application process, ensuring a tailored approach to each individual's abilities and interests. Passionate about sharing their experiences, our associates are committed to helping aspiring talents succeed worldwide.

With over 60 years of combined experience in education and career counseling, our team is dedicated to leveraging our expertise for the benefit of the younger generation. We specialize in guiding students to secure prestigious international scholarships for overseas education at premier universities worldwide. Our extensive network of partner universities spans the UK, USA, EU countries, Malaysia, Turkey, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, Middle East, Central, and Asian countries. Years of dedicated effort have allowed us to successfully place numerous students in top universities abroad.
